About Us

About Us

Peace and Plants began from a mutual desire to share our journey into veganism and spread the love to anyone interested. We are Devin and Julio, vegan since July 2019. We started the transition for a compilation of health benefits, to advocate for animal rights, and to combat climate change. We want to be able to create a better world for the generations that follow us.

We have actually felt and seen the drastic health benefits of eliminating meat, dairy, and other animal products from our diets. We both agree we have more energy, less fatigue/sluggish feelings after meals, and a more regulated digestive system. We have also seen the results through our regular lab results noting reduced cholesterol levels and A1C levels. Devin has also seen a much welcomed reduction in facial and body acne. We also maintain a moral standard that all living things deserve respect and kindness. We believe animals should be given the opportunity to live out the life that nature would allow them to live. Commercial animal agriculture has become a gruesome industry that is not actually necessary for humans to survive and thrive. We chose to do our best to not support this industry. We also understand the enormous affect the animal agriculture industry has on the deterioration of our current climate. This is another process that we are not willing to support financially.

All this to say, this is who we are and why we support a vegan lifestyle. We want to help ease your transition to veganism if you're interested and are always open to any questions or concerns you may have. Peace, everybody!
